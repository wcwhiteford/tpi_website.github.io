Services

Services

  • Municipal Services

    Team Plan offers a full array of out-sourced public sector services, incuding local government Planning & Zoning Adminstration, permit review, and site plan approval.

  • Entitlement Services

    Rezonings, PUD's, development order amendments, conditional uses, special exceptions, variances, project management, and agent representation.

  • Comprehensive Planning

    Plan updates, Plan amendments, Evaluation and Appraisal Reports (EAR's), major and minor map changes, annexation reports, data and analysis.

  • Zoning

    Zoning Code reform, updates, text changes, map amendments (rezonings), interpertations, expert-witness testimony, graphics and illustrations.

  • GIS

    Geographic Information System (GIS) support, maps, data and analysis, including Comprehensive Plan Map Series & publicly accessible ftp sites.

  • Master Planning

    Master planning services, including residential and non-residential planned developments, mixed-use developments, commerce and technology centers.

  • Site Planning

    Site planning services, including residential, commercial, industrial, recreational, institutional, and mixed uses. Site plan amendments and code variances.

  • Subdivision

    Preliminary subdivision plans, plats, and replats, including subdivision variances, right-of-way abandonment applications, and plat waivers.

  • LEED

    LEED Neighborhood Development (LEED-ND) and LEED Building Design & Construction (LEED-BD&C) professionally accredited and certified by the USGBC.

  • Due Diligence

    Land use and zoning consistency, utility and traffic availability, adequate public facilities (concurrency) determinations, and development potential scenerio plans.

Futurescapes

Futurescapes is the methodology developed by Team Plan to create 3D scenerios of future development using population and employment projections, parcel-based land use suitability analysis, and the principles of GeoDesign. Futurescapes result in better information to the public and better decisions by policymakers.

Save the Mounds

SavetheMounds.org is the non-profit 501.3(c) created by Team Plan to catalog and record every known Native American midden in the State of Florida. We do this using LiDAR technology and GIS to create 3D images of existing conditions to better preserve our hertitage for future generations.
